Anime Expo (Los Angeles, CA)

North America's largest anime convention, Anime Expo brings together fans, creators, and industry professionals from around the world. Enjoy exclusive announcements, premiere screenings, and panels featuring top Japanese creators. Highlights include a massive exhibition hall with unique merchandise, cosplay contests, artist alleys, and live musical performances by renowned Japanese artists.

Date: 4 July 2026

Otakon (Washington, DC)

Held at the Walter E. Washington Convention Center, Otakon is one of the largest conventions on the East Coast dedicated to anime, manga, and East Asian pop culture. With thousands of attendees every year, the event features guest panels, gaming tournaments, a dedicated dealers room, and vibrant cosplay gatherings celebrating both classic and modern series.

Date: 1 August 2026

Past Events

Anime NYC

Anime NYC is New York City's premier anime convention, held annually at the Javits Center. The event introduces Japanese animation, gaming, and pop culture to fans across the northeastern United States. Attendees enjoy exclusive industry announcements, meet-and-greets with beloved voice actors, cultural exhibits, and a bustling exhibition floor packed with rare collectibles and fan-made art.

Date: 21 November 2025

Sakura-Con (Seattle, WA)

Sakura-Con, held in Seattle at the Washington State Convention Center, is one of the Pacific Northwest's most beloved non-profit anime conventions. Run entirely by volunteers for fans, it features an impressive lineup of Japanese music performances, voice actor Q&A panels, a spectacular cosplay parade, and a bustling dealers hall with exclusive merchandise.

What sets Sakura-Con apart is its welcoming philosophy — once inside, all panels, performances, and autograph sessions are included with admission. From intimate fan meetups to large-scale stage events, every moment reflects the passion of a community built on a genuine love of Japanese culture and anime.

Date: 18 April 2025
