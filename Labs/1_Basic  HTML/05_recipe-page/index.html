Spicy Garlic Butter Noodles

A quick and flavorful noodle dish made with buttery garlic sauce, a hint of spice, and simple pantry ingredients. Perfect for a fast lunch or late-night craving.

Ingredients

  • 200g noodles (any type)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 4 cloves garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles according to package instructions and drain them.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add chili flakes and soy sauce, mixing well.
  4. Toss in the cooked noodles and stir until evenly coated.
  5. Adjust salt, mix well, and serve hot.
